# Privacy Policy — ShieldKey 2FA Authenticator

**Last updated:** June 2026

## Summary

ShieldKey does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. All information is stored locally on your device.

## Data Storage

ShieldKey stores the following data locally using Chrome's built-in storage API:

- TOTP secret keys (required to generate 2FA codes)
- Account labels and service names
- User preferences (theme setting, account order)

This data never leaves your browser. It is not sent to any server, analytics service, or third party.

## Data Collection

ShieldKey does **not** collect:

- Personal information
- Browsing history
- Location data
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports
- Any other user data

## Permissions

- **storage** — To save your accounts and preferences locally
- **activeTab** — To scan QR codes on the current page (only when you click "Scan")
- **scripting** — To inject the QR scanner into the active tab (only when you trigger a scan)
- **clipboardWrite** — To copy 2FA codes when you click an account

## Third Parties

ShieldKey does not use any third-party services, APIs, or analytics. No network requests are made by this extension.

## Open Source

The complete source code is publicly available for review on GitHub.
